Community Guidelines

These guidelines help keep our community safe, respectful, and helpful for everyone navigating their health journey.

  • 💬
    Share experiences, not medical advice

    You can share what worked for you, but always remind others to consult their care team before making health decisions.

  • 🤝
    Be kind — everyone's journey is different

    Treat others with empathy. Chronic illness affects everyone differently; avoid judgment and comparisons.

  • 🔒
    Protect your privacy

    Avoid sharing full names, location details, or other identifiers that could identify you or others.

  • ⚕️
    Always consult your care team

    Community discussions are peer support, not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.
