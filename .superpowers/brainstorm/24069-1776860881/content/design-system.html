Open Decision: Per-User-Type Accent Colors?

Each user type could share the exact same palette (one brand) — or get a subtle accent shift that makes their portal feel tailored to their context. Both are defensible.

A

One Unified Palette

All four user types share teal + navy. Differentiation comes from layout density and content, not color. Stronger brand coherence, simpler design system to maintain.

B

Shared Core + Role Accents

Core teal/navy brand is constant. Each role gets a secondary accent: Patient (warm teal), NGO (amber), HMO (cool blue), Researcher (violet). Users immediately feel "this portal is mine."
