Other Role Onboarding — Step Structure

🤝 NGO — 4 Steps
1. Account (contact person + credentials)
2. Organisation (name, reg. no., focus areas)
3. Geographic scope + documentation upload
4. Consent + verification pending notice
⚠ NGO accounts require admin verification before activation
🏦 HMO — 4 Steps
1. Account (admin contact)
2. Organisation (name, licence no., coverage region)
3. BAA acknowledgement (HIPAA)
4. Consent + verification pending notice
⚠ HMO accounts require admin verification before activation
🔬 Researcher — 4 Steps
1. Account (name, credentials)
2. Institution + research focus areas
3. IRB/ethics board affiliation (optional)
4. Consent + platform liability disclaimer
⚠ Researcher accounts require admin verification before activation
