Design Direction

Three distinct visual personalities for Lucen Care — which feels right?

LUCEN CARE
Healthcare access,
built on trust.
Typography: Libre Baskerville + Inter

A — Clinical Authority

Deep navy, serif headings, structured layouts. Signals institutional trust and credibility. Strong for HMOs and Clinical Researchers. Risk: may feel cold or inaccessible to patients.

Lucen Care
Your health journey,
supported every step.
Typography: Nunito + Nunito Sans

B — Warm & Human

Terracotta, warm sand, sage green. Rounded forms, friendly type. Immediately approachable for patients and caregivers. Risk: may feel too lifestyle-y for B2B users (HMOs, researchers).

LUCEN CARE
Connecting care.
Enabling outcomes.
Typography: DM Sans + DM Mono accents

C — Modern Healthtech

Deep teal, electric green accent, dark navy. Clean, tech-forward, data-literate. Works equally well across all four user types. Feels like a platform built to scale. Risk: needs careful execution not to feel generic SaaS.
