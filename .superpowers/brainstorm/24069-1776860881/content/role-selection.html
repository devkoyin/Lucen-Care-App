A — 2×2 grid, users see all four roles at once, each card is self-contained and clickable. Clean, parallel, fast.
B — Vertical list with radio-select interaction. One clear CTA updates as you select. More guided, clearer affordance, better for users who hesitate.
My recommendation: B — the confirmation step ("Continue as Patient →") reduces accidental wrong-role sign-ups which are annoying to recover from.
